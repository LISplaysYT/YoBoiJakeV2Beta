Encountering an Error?

If you click on a game or app and it gives you the error message: TypeError: Failed to fetch, try one of the following:

  • Refresh the page and try again.
  • Exit the game or app and go back in after a few moments.
  • If the problem keeps happening keep doing it until it works it should work by the 3 try if not keep trying.
